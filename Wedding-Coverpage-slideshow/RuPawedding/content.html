Our Story

Nobody had the slightest idea what it would be like, when it all started...Parul, was too excited to start working in Bangalore. Rutwik wasn't very eager to leave home in Mumbai and start working yet. Unaware of the other, both finally met in Accenture at Bangalore.

Rutwik was smitten from the start, but for first few months reserved himself to the fact that she was out of his league. They stayed friends during this time which was filled with lots of laughter, fun, house parties, games and travel. And as the friendship grew, they knew it was also...love.

Over the past 3 years, they have pushed each other to be their best, challenged each other on almost everything (sometimes just for the heck of it) and learned to love through it all. Now they can't wait to spend the rest of their lives together.
